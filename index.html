Type Romanized Nepali words in the box below and the words will be converted into unicode Nepali as you press the spacebar.

Example: typing "hami nepali bhashi" becomes "हामी नेपाली भाषी".

  • » Use the backspace or click on any words to get more choices.
  • » Click अ button below or press Ctrl+G to toggle between English and Nepali.

  • » Highlight to select, copy and paste where needed.
  • » Hard to type letters and full stop can be copied from the list below.
